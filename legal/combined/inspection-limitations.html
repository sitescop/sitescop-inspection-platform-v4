Inspection Limitations

This inspection is a visual assessment only and is limited to areas that are reasonably accessible at the time of inspection. The inspection is conducted without dismantling, removing, damaging or disturbing any part of the property.

The following limitations apply to this inspection:

  • Furniture, floor coverings, stored goods and personal belongings are not moved.
  • Wall linings, ceiling linings, insulation, floor coverings and finishes are not removed.
  • The inspection does not include destructive testing unless separately agreed in writing.
  • Areas that are unsafe or inaccessible cannot be inspected.
  • Roof spaces and subfloors may not be accessible where insufficient clearance or safety risks exist.
  • Heavy rain, poor lighting or weather conditions may affect inspection findings.
  • Underground services, concealed plumbing, concealed electrical wiring and hidden structural components are outside the scope of this inspection.
  • Swimming pools, spas, retaining walls, outbuildings and detached structures are only inspected where specifically included.

No inspection can guarantee that every defect or timber pest issue will be detected because some conditions may be concealed or may develop after the inspection date.

The inspection report represents the condition of the property only at the time of the inspection.
