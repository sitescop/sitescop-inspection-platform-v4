Scope of Inspection

This agreement applies to a Combined Building and Timber Pest Inspection requested by the Client and carried out by SiteScop Pty Ltd.

The inspection consists of two separate inspections performed during the same visit:

  • Building Inspection in accordance with Australian Standard AS 4349.1.
  • Timber Pest Inspection in accordance with Australian Standard AS 4349.3.

The purpose of the inspection is to identify the condition of the property at the time of inspection and report on any significant defects, safety hazards, timber pest activity, timber pest damage, conditions conducive to timber pest attack and other relevant observations that are visible and reasonably accessible.

The inspection is a visual assessment only and is non-destructive. The inspector will not dismantle, remove, damage or disturb any part of the building, finishes, fixtures, furniture or stored items.

The inspection includes all reasonably accessible areas of the property, including where safe and accessible:

  • Exterior
  • Interior
  • Roof Exterior
  • Roof Void
  • Subfloor
  • Garage
  • Outbuildings where included
  • Site improvements

Areas that are unsafe, inaccessible, obstructed or concealed cannot be inspected and will be identified within the report where applicable.

The inspection report reflects the condition of the property only at the date and time of inspection. No warranty is given that the condition of the property will remain unchanged after the inspection.

This inspection is intended to assist the Client in making an informed decision regarding the property. It should be read in its entirety together with all photographs, notes, recommendations and limitations contained within the report.
