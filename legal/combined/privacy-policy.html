Privacy Policy

SiteScop Pty Ltd ("SiteScop", "we", "our" or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy and handling your personal information responsibly. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, store and disclose your personal information when you engage our inspection services.

1. Information We Collect

Depending on the services requested, we may collect personal information including:

  • Full name.
  • Residential or postal address.
  • Property inspection address.
  • Mobile and telephone numbers.
  • Email address.
  • Real estate agent details.
  • Property owner details where supplied.
  • Inspection booking information.
  • Electronic signatures.
  • Inspection photographs and related documents.
  • Payment and invoicing information.

2. Purpose of Collection

Your personal information is collected for the purpose of:

  • Providing building and timber pest inspection services.
  • Preparing inspection reports.
  • Managing inspection bookings.
  • Communicating with you regarding inspections.
  • Issuing invoices and receipts.
  • Responding to enquiries.
  • Meeting legal and regulatory obligations.
  • Improving our services and customer experience.

3. Disclosure of Information

SiteScop will not sell, rent or trade your personal information.

Your information may only be disclosed where reasonably necessary to:

  • Provide the requested inspection service.
  • Issue reports to authorised recipients.
  • Process payments.
  • Comply with Australian laws.
  • Respond to lawful requests from government authorities.

4. Storage and Security

Reasonable administrative, technical and physical safeguards are used to protect personal information from unauthorised access, disclosure, alteration or loss.

Inspection reports, photographs, agreements and associated documents may be stored electronically using secure business systems and cloud-based services used by SiteScop.

5. Access and Correction

You may request access to the personal information we hold about you or request correction of inaccurate information, subject to applicable Australian privacy laws.

6. Cookies and Website Usage

Where our website is used to request inspections or complete agreements, basic technical information such as browser type, device information, IP address and website usage data may be collected to improve system performance, security and user experience.

7. Electronic Communications

By engaging SiteScop, you consent to receiving communications relating to your inspection by email, SMS or telephone, including inspection confirmations, invoices, reminders, reports and follow-up correspondence.

8. Retention of Records

Inspection reports, agreements, signatures, photographs and related records may be retained for the period required by law, professional obligations or legitimate business purposes.

9. Privacy Enquiries

If you have any questions regarding this Privacy Policy or the handling of your personal information, please contact SiteScop Pty Ltd using the contact details provided on our website.

10. Client Consent

By accepting this Agreement and providing your personal information, you consent to the collection, use, storage and disclosure of your information in accordance with this Privacy Policy and applicable Australian privacy legislation.
